Frequently Asked Questions

Can this platform integrate with our existing Core Banking System (CBS)?
Yes. Our platform is built on a microservices architecture with open APIs. We seamlessly integrate with Finacle, BaNCS, Flexcube, and other major CBS providers.
How does the AI Underwriting engine work?
The engine uses OCR to read documents, pulls data via API from NSDL, UIDAI, CIBIL for credit scores, and Account Aggregators for banking history to calculate FOIR in seconds.
How long does it take to deploy the white-label platform?
We offer rapid deployment. Once the branding and API credentials are provided, the platform can go live for your bank or NBFC in less than 30 days.
Is the platform compliant with RBI data localization norms?
Absolutely. All data is hosted on highly secure, ISO 27001 certified servers physically located within India, fully adhering to RBI and UIDAI compliance guidelines.
